ATMs

Word of Advice: It is better to bring extra cash than to bring too little.

Mexican ATMs do accept American credit/debit cards. However, Bank of Mexico takes a 10% TAX on every non-Mexican transaction. For example, if you withdraw $200 from your Bank of America debit card, $220 will be withdrawn from your account.

Clubbing

Before Clubbing

  • The legal drinking age in Cancun, Mexico is 18+.
  • As in most cases, alcoholic beverages are most expensive at the clubs.
    • Solution: Pre-game at your hotel!
  • You need your ID.
    • Solution: You can take a picture of your ID on your phone (Passport, driver's license, other identification that will verify your age) and just show the picture at the entrance of the club.

At the Club

"Dicks have to tip for drinks" - ladies can disregard this!

  • This is for the guys... even if you paid for that all-inclusive VIP club pass, it is almost impossible to get drinks at the club.
    • Still want that drink? Tip your bartender. It's the only way.

Club VIP Passes

  • Gives you access to a table and some drinks: a couple bottles of vodka, chaser, and water.
  • Our personal advice: We do not think VIP passes are worth their price.
    • Once you are sufficiently intoxicated, bartenders will try to make you pay them unreasonable tips! Some even threatened to take our table away if we did not tip them. No matter how unreasonable you feel the argument is, there is little you can do to dissuade them.

Hotels

Wi-Fi

More often than not, you will have to pay to use Wi-Fi :(

Price range: $10-16 / night

Private Rentals

Hotel prices can be overwhelming on your budget. If you have a group size of 5+ people, we highly recommend that you stay at a private rental houe. It is comparable less expensive than staying at a hotel.

BUT Private rentals usually do not provide all-inclusive services (unless the house is affiliated with a hotel).

Alcohol

Want to casually drink tax-free, personally-selected liquor in your rooms? You can in Cancun!

How? Buy it at the Duty Free Shop, located in the Cancun airport (right next to the baggage claim).
